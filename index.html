Common Patterns Identified

Strategic Alignment

Focus on aligning content with brand voice, marketing objectives, and audience personas across all levels.

Performance-Driven

Heavy emphasis on measuring success using data, evaluating engagement metrics, and iterating content.

Multi-Platform Versatility

Expectation to be "platform-native" understanding nuances of TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Web.

Collaborative Workflows

Cross-functional liaison between designers, copywriters, technical teams, and external stakeholders.

Core Skills (Non-Negotiable)

  • Writing & Storytelling: Copywriting, editing, and narrative development.
  • SEO & Analytics: Keyword research, organic traffic generation, and data interpretation.
  • Visual Literacy: Conceptualizing graphic/video assets aligned with brand.
  • Content Moderation: Judging content quality, legality, and policy compliance.

Complementary Skills (Power Skills)

  • AI Literacy: Prompt engineering (ChatGPT, Midjourney) for workflow speed.
  • Project Management: Handling timelines, budgets, and team bandwidth.
  • Consumer Psychology: Understanding behavioral triggers for engagement.
  • Leadership: Mentoring junior creators and maintaining creative standards.
